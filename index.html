Canvas Export

How to create a Canvas Access Token:
1. Log in to your Canvas account on a computer. Your mobile phone may not work correctly.
2. Click your profile icon in the top left (Account).
3. Select Settings.
4. Scroll down to the “Approved Integrations” section.
5. Click + New Access Token.
6. (Optional) Add a purpose and expiration date.
7. Click Generate Token and copy the token. Paste this into a place that you can access like an email or word doc. You will not be able to access this from the canvas site again. You can generate a new token if needed.
8. Paste it above in the “Canvas Access Token” field.
